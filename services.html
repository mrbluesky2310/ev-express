Battery & Motor Diagnostics

Our certified technicians perform full battery health assessments using industry-grade diagnostic equipment. We measure cell voltage, state of charge, thermal performance, and motor efficiency. Early detection prevents costly failures and extends your EV's operational lifespan.

Scope of Work
  • Cell voltage & SoC analysis
  • Thermal imaging scan
  • Motor torque diagnostics
  • BMS check
  • Health report

Routine Maintenance

Regular maintenance is essential for EV longevity on Cambodia's roads. Our service covers all manufacturer-recommended inspection points tailored to local conditions. We ensure brakes, cooling systems, and electrical connections meet safety standards.

Scope of Work
  • Brake inspection
  • Coolant top-up & flush
  • Tyre rotation
  • Electrical connector check
  • Cabin filter replacement

Software & Firmware Updates

EV manufacturers regularly release firmware to improve performance and safety. Our technicians apply official OTA and manual updates for all major brands in Cambodia. Keeping software current optimises charging efficiency and driver-assistance features.

Scope of Work
  • OTA update installation
  • BMS recalibration
  • ADAS reconfiguration
  • Infotainment updates
  • Post-update validation

Mobile Roadside Assistance

Breakdowns don't follow business hours and neither do we. Our 24/7 mobile units reach your location anywhere in Phnom Penh within the hour. Our technicians resolve most issues on-site without a tow.

Scope of Work
  • Emergency charging
  • Tyre change
  • Electrical fault repair
  • Jump-start service
  • Towing coordination

Fleet Service Contracts

Corporate and government EV fleets benefit from structured proactive maintenance agreements. We schedule servicing around your operational calendar to minimise downtime. Every contract includes a dedicated account manager and monthly health reporting.

Scope of Work
  • Multi-vehicle scheduled servicing
  • Monthly fleet health reports
  • Priority SLA response
  • Driver training
  • Custom terms for 5+ vehicles

Technician Training Partnership

Cambodia needs qualified EV technicians to support its rapidly growing fleet. Our training academy delivers certified courses designed for real-world Cambodian conditions. Graduates receive internationally recognised credentials.

Scope of Work

  • Corporate group packages
