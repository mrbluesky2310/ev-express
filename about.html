Our Story

Built for Cambodia's EV Future

Cambodia's EV Market is Growing Fast

Cambodia's electric vehicle landscape has transformed at remarkable speed. From just 313 EV registrations in 2023, the country's EV fleet surged to 15,239 registered vehicles by June 2026 — a near 50× increase in under three years.

Yet despite this explosive growth, the qualified service infrastructure has not kept pace. Most EV owners in Phnom Penh and beyond are left without access to certified diagnostics, genuine replacement parts, or trained technicians who truly understand modern electric drivetrains.

EV Express Cambodia was founded to close this gap — bringing certified EV expertise, genuine parts, and professional servicing to every Cambodian EV owner and fleet operator.

Our Vision

To be Cambodia's most trusted electric vehicle service network, setting the standard for EV safety, reliability, and expertise.

Our Mission

To keep Cambodia's growing fleet of electric vehicles running safely and efficiently by providing certified diagnostics, genuine parts, and expert technician training.

Our Growth Roadmap

Year 1 Launch
First Certified EV Garage in Phnom Penh

Establish our flagship diagnostics lab, hire 5 certified EV technicians, and launch the online parts store — giving Cambodia its first dedicated, accredited EV service centre.

Phase 1 — Foundation
Year 2 Expand
Mobile Roadside & Regional Branches

Launch a mobile roadside assistance fleet to serve EV owners on the go. Open service branches in Siem Reap & Sihanoukville, and grow the team to 15+ certified technicians nationwide.

Phase 2 — Growth
Year 3 Scale
EV Technician Training Academy

Open the EV Technician Training Academy to upskill Cambodia's automotive workforce. Establish franchise and partner garages nationwide, reaching 50+ certified technicians across the country.
